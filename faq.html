What options are there for bringing gifts?

In lieu of gifts, we are instead accepting donations for our local food bank, the Bread Run

Does the Bread Run accept monetary donations?

A volunteer will be stationed at the entrance of the church to assist with monetary donations. We will have a debit machine setup, as well as other means by which to donate. Cash will also be accepted.

Can I bring food donations instead?

Absolutely! We accept any non-perishable, non-expired, shelf stable donations. Please see this list of excellent options that are always in very high demand for the food bank:

Donated food will be sitting on a table at room temperature overnight, so please bare this in mind for donated items!

  • Shelf Stable Vegetables (Potatoes, Carrots, etc)
  • Easily divisible, packaged toiletries (Menstrual products, toilet paper rolls, toothpastes, deoderants, etc. The "travel" sections of stores can be an excellent option to get many small divisible items for distributing)
  • Canned foods (beans, fish, mussels, soups, lentils, etc)
  • Peanut Butter
  • Dry goods (Cereals, Pastas, instant potatoes, oatmeal)
  • Diapers

What's the Paper Airplane Contest?

We will be handing out a sheet of A4 size paper to every guest. They will be encouraged to quickly make the best paper airplane that they can. Then we all as a group will line up and throw our paper airplanes to see who's can fly the farthest (A photo will be taken to commemorate the event too).

We highly recommend folks practice their folding skills beforehand, bring your A game!

What is the Hand Unfasting?

The Bride and Groom will be participating in the ritual of Hand Fasting during our ceremony.

For the duration of time between the first ceremony, and the second Unfasting Ceremony later, their hands will be tied together by a ceremonial rope.

The Unfasting Ceremony will be a much shorter small ceremony where the rope is removed, signifying the final step of their union

What other sorts of games and challenges will be happening?

During the Games and Challenges period of the reception, the Bride and Groom will still be Hand Fasted together. We are asking guests to submit ahead of time possible fun challenges to have the Bride and Groom attempt while their hands are still tied together! See our contact info here

What will "Board Games" involve?

After the event is over at 2:00PM and the food bank receive is complete, we will be setting up for some chill board games with friends, everyone is welcome to join, we have plenty of tables and chairs we can setup for folks to visit, relax, and play some games.

Do I have to participate in the Board Games after?

Not at all, if you just wanna hang out and visit, or if you have to head out, we totally understand.
